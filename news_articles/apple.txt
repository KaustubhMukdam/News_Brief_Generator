Title: Apple Unveils New iPhone
Text: Apple has announced the release of the iPhone 16 at its September event in California. The device features a new 200MP camera, an AI-powered battery manager, and satellite connectivity. CEO Tim Cook stated that the phone is “the smartest, greenest iPhone yet.” Initial reviews have praised the improved battery life and camera performance. The iPhone 16 will be available for pre-order starting October 1, and will ship to stores October 15.
