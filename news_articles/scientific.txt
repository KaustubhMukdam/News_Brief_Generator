Title: Discovery of Water on Mars
Text: NASA scientists have confirmed the presence of liquid water on Mars. Using satellite imagery, they found dark streaks on the surface that expand and shrink depending on the season, a sign of briny water flows. This discovery increases the possibility that life could exist on Mars and is seen as a major milestone for future space missions. The announcement has generated excitement in the science community and could pave the way for human exploration of the Red Planet.
