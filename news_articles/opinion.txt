Title: Why Remote Work Is Here To Stay
Text: In the past three years, remote work has evolved from a temporary solution to a norm for millions. Companies enjoy increased productivity and lower costs, but employees also benefit from better work-life balance and geographic flexibility. Critics argue it hampers collaboration and company culture, yet most evidence suggests that remote work will continue to grow, with hybrid models leading the way. In this new era, companies must rethink communication and support systems to succeed.
